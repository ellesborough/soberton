Married John 24th January 1831 in St Peter's Church, Soberton.  
 1841 and 1851 lived in Greenfield, Soberton, 1861 lived on Cot Street, 1871 still lived in the Droxford area  
 By 1881 she was widowed and living alone in Soberton, working as a Dressmaker.  
 On the 1891 census she is still living alone in Soberton, this time as a (sick) Nurse.  
   
 She had 10 children:  
 Thomas (1832), Ann (1834), John (1837), Maria (1840), May (1841), Sarah (1844), William (1846), Emma (1848), Elizabeth (1850), Charles (1854)
