Baptised (26 Oct 1856) and grew up in Petersfield. Married Samuel in East Meon, then moved to Soberton with their 5 children. In total they had 11 children; the eldest, [ Samuel ](http://soberton.hol.es/node/818) is buried at St Peter's and the youngest son [ Harry ](http://soberton.hol.es/node/1053) died in WWI and is commemorated on the Soberton War Memorial.  
   
 Although census lists birth as being at Sheet, Hampshire, no Hampshire records found, however plausible birth record of a Martha Benham at Lambeth, Q2/1855
