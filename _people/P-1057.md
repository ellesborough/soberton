Married Maria Easden at St Peter's Church, Soberton on the 24th January 1831   
 1841 census lists him, Maria, and 4 children at Greenfield, Soberton and he was a Shepherd. In 1851 they still lived at Greenfield, with 7 children. The 1861 census lists him with wife Maria and his 4 younger children, living at Cot Street, Soberton. Next door was [ Thomas Bucksey ](http://soberton.hol.es/node/833), 25 years his junior, presumed to be his son.  
 Death record age doesn't exactly agree with 1861 census age.  
 Grandson [ John ](http://soberton.hol.es/node/1061) was killed in WW1.
