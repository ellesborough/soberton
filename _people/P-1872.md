Son of William and Lucy Davies, of Tillington, Hereford.  
 He married Eleanor Lavina Mitchell on the 22nd February 1902.   
 He joined the Royal Navy during WWI as a Stoker Petty Officer, he was killed during the Battle of Jutland 31st May 1916, on HMS Queen Mary.   
 He and Eleanor had four children: [Lavinia](/node/1803), Lucia, Thomas, and Alfred (who was born only a month before he died). The family lived at Furze Cottage, Soberton.   
 He is commemorated on the Portsmouth Naval Memorial. [CWWG](https://www.cwgc.org/find-records/find-war-dead/casualty-details/2876431/thomas-george-davies/)
