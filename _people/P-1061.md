Born in Soberton, the sixth of eleven children.  
 The 1891 census lists the family on Hambledon Lane, Soberton, his father was a shepherd. By the 1901 census the family lived at Stoneridge, Catherington, and at age 14 was working as a Shepherd Boy.  
 By the 1911 census the family lived at East Hoe Cottages, Soberton and he was working as a Shepherd.  
 He died in WWI serving with 2nd Battalion, Hampshire Regiment in France;
