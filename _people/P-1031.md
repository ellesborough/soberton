Died at Ypres, during WW1, serving as a Corporal with 7th (overseas) battalion, Canadian Infantry.  
 "The Second Battle of Ypres began in April 1915 when the Germans released poison gas into the Allied lines north of Ypres. This was the first time gas had been used by either side"  
 Commemorated at Menin Gate Memorial, Belgium  
 From CWGC: *Son of the late John and Mary Rachel Twynam, late of Soberton House, Soberton, Hants., England*  
 Commemorated on Soberton War Memorial and on plaque in the church;  
 Also Served in the 2nd Boer War in the Cape Mounted Riflemen in South Africa
