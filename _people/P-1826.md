Only child of [Oliver Nehemiah](http://soberton.hol.es/node/1014) and Margaret Messam. Her father died shortly after she was born in WW1 and her mother died in 1934.   
 Baptised 8th Jan 1919 in St Jude, Peckham, Southwark, London, with the registered address as 35 York Grove.  
 Died aged 25 at "The Civil Isolation Hospital" Northdown, Aldershot, with her address listed as Waterside, Droxford.  
   
 Presume "Babs" was just a nickname, not related to the name Barbara.
