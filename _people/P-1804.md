Born 19th Sept 1900, Youngest of the 4 children of James and Mary Ann.  
 Listed on the 1901 census with his family as "Arthur", living on Liberty Road \[interestingly there seems to have been some confusion by the numerator about whether this was in Soberton or Newtown...\]. The 1911 census lists the family at Forest Lodge, Newtown, and he is listed under his full name "Percy Arthur".  
 Married Elizabeth Annie Evelyn White in late 1926 in the Droxford district. (she was born: 7th Jan 1906)  
 They are both listed on the 1939 register living at 2 High Croft Soberton Heath; he is working as a Private Groom.
