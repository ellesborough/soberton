Found on 1891 census, aged 1, living with parents and grandparents at The Lawns, Springfield, Chelmsford, Essex, with 7 domestic staff.  
 Known as Lindsay Jackson  
 Can't find certain marriage record  
 Awarded Distinguished Service Order in 1918 for "Services in Monitors and Destroyers of the Dover Patrol"  
 Was captain of [H.M.S. Despatch](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/HMS_Despatch_(D30)) 1935-1937.  
 Page about him on [Dreadnought Project](http://dreadnoughtproject.org/tfs/index.php/William_Lindsay_Jackson)  
 Pictures at [National Portrait Gallery](http://www.npg.org.uk/collections/search/person/mp83011/william-lindsay-jackson)  
 During WW2, he was stationed in Ankara, as a [Naval Atache to Turkey](https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=-Ki5EaA14sQC&pg=PA178&lpg=PA178&dq=rear+admiral+lindsay+jackson&source=bl&ots=b9qF_8lgmH&sig=Y0ICKisLLFO8D-Pmb0nRCoBAERA&hl=en&sa=X&ved=0ahUKEwiUj4ql3avSAhXhDMAKHTAcDPEQ6AEIUDAP#v=onepage&q=rear%20admiral%20lindsay%20jackson&f=false)
