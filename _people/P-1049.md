Born in Hambledon as the second of four sons, Brother to [Joseph Longman](/node/1011)   
 In 1901 the census the family lived in the Poor Cottages on Inglefield Lane, Soberton, with his father working as a Ground Labourer. By the 1911 census the family lived at Whitedale Farm, Hambledon with his father working as a Cowman on the farm.  
 Thomas died in WW1 of his wounds in France at the [NO 1 GENERAL HOSPITAL B.E.F. ETRETAT](http://familyatwar.co.uk/index.php/home/etretat/) serving with the 15th Battalion Hampshire regiment  
   
 [ CWGC ](https://www.cwgc.org/find-records/find-war-dead/casualty-details/101751/thomas-longman/)  *Son of Joseph and Rosa Longman, of Soberton Heath*   
 He is commemorated on Soberton War Memorial
