Memorial inside St. Peter's Soberton  
 *The memorial of Charles Richard Pink FRIBA was placed in this church, restored under his direction by some of his friends in the Architectural Association London who liked his personality and recognised his ability and character. He was present of the Architectural Association 1885-1886. Born in this parish July 4th 1853, died at Hyde near Winchester 25th Feb 1889*
