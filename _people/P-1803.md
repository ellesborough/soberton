Eldest of the four children of Eleanor and [Thomas Davies](/node/1872). Her father was killed during the Battle of Jutland. The family lived at Furze Cottage, Soberton.  
   
 She married Joseph W Longman in Droxford in 1925. They had a daughter - Ruth.  
 1939 Register lists the family living on Chapel Road, Soberton Heath, with her occupation as Unpaid Domestic Duties.  
   
 Her date of death is not listed on the grave, but her age would suggest a date of death around 1990-91, although no death record can be found.
