Born the eldest of their 3 daughters to Charles and Betsey \[Elizabeth\] Sarah Bower; She was baptised 7th August 1842 at St Mary's Church, Southampton. By the 1851 census lists the family were living at 36 Havant Street, Portsea (her father was a Stone Mason). The 1861 census lists the family at the same address, but her father is a General dealer, and her maternal grandmother (Mary Ann Hornet) was also living at this address (she was previously a Shop Keeper).   
 Mary Ann Bowers married Edward Wyatt at St Thomas Church, Portsmouth 8th May 1862   
 The 1871 census lists them at 38 Fyning Street, Portsea with 2 daughters (Elizabeth and Mary G). By 1881 the census lists them at 17 Havant Street, Portsea. Edward is now a pensioner (Greenwich). The 1891 census lists them at 7 Stamshaw Road, Portsea; also living with them is their daughter Elizabeth S and her husband Thomas Mills, who is a Royal Marine.  
 By 1901 the census lists them living in Soberton Heath. Also with them is Mary Gertrude Anderson (their married daughter), and three grandchildren (Lilian, Donald, John).  
 1911 census lists them at Yew Tree Cottage, Soberton; also in the household is Donald Anderson (Grandson by Mary) who is a Telegraph Boy for the Post Office; and Elizabeth Roland (Mary's widowed sister)
