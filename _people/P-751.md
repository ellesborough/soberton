Born in Hythe, Hampshire as the second child of Edward and Mary Ann Wyatt  
 The 1871 census lists Edward and Mary Ann at 38 Fyning Street, Portsea with 2 daughters: Elizabeth (5) and Mary G (3). By 1881 census lists the family at 17 Havant Street, Portsea.   
   
 Mary Gertrude Wyatt marries John Robert Anderson 2 Sept 1893 in Portsea. They must have lived in Kent for some time as this is where Donald was born, however, by 1901 census lists her living in Soberton Heath with her parents, and their three grandchildren (Lilian (1895), Donald (1897), John (1908)), (husband) John was away at sea.
