1891 Census has him living as a widower aged 78 at 37 Brunswick Square, Hove, with his son Charles, his wife Anne Argentine and their two children Argentine and Edith.   
 They had 4 live-in maids and a butler.  
 24 Brunswick Square is where Peter Rogers' great-great grandfather Henry Smith was listed as a servant for George Ovrest on 1861 census. His daughter-in-law Margaret Follen was also listed as a parlourmaid at 21 Brunswick Place for Dr. William Kebbell.
